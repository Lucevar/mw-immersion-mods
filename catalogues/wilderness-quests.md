# Wilderness Quests 

# Ascadian Isles Quests
#### A Man and His Guar
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
If both survive, Rollie and Teris Raledran will relocate to Balmora upon completion of the quest. You will also have to escort Teris into Agrippina Herennia's shop for the quest to complete (opposed to outside the shop as in vanilla MW).
#### An Escort to Molag Mar
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
Paur Maston and Vanjirra are disabled if you refuse to escort Paur or if either one of them dies. They will both relocate to The Pilgrim's Rest to conduct their business upon completion of the quest.
#### Nels Llendo
#### The Angry Trader
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
Tinos Drothan will relocate to The Flowers of Gold upon completion of the quest, otherwise will be disabled if you refuse the quest.
#### The Beauty and the Bandit
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
Happily ever after? Heartbreak and misery? Somewhere in between? Find out what happens with Maurrie Aurmine and Nelos Onmar!
#### The Scholars and the Mating Kagouti
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
Edras Onil and Thoronor will relocate to Desele's House of Earthly Delights (much to Edras' chagrin) upon completion of the quest. If either of them dies, the other will be disabled. The player will also give Edras his notes that were dropped ("Notes - Kagouti Mating Habits") if in your possession during the quest (given to Thoronor if Edras dies before giving the notes to him).
#### To the Fields of Kummu
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
Nevrasa Dralor will relocate to Suran Temple upon completion of the quest.
#### Tul's Escape
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
Tul is relocated to the guardhouse on Dren Plantation if Sterdecan is already dead or killed during the quest.

# Ashlands Quests
#### Divided by Nix Hounds
* [**Quest Tweaks & Alternatives 1.19**](https://www.dropbox.com/s/0ihtlpfrzfhiwxo/QTA_1.19.7z?dl=0) by Stuporstar  
Now gives you one chance to accept the plea of the husband if you refuse the wife. If you refuse outright, one of them will die and the other will show up in town. Your actions here will affect disposition with all of Ald-Ruhn. If you save them, they'll both move to the Ald Skar Inn.
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
Drerel and Falanu Indaren will relocate to the Ald Skar Inn upon completion of the quest; Drerel will be killed and Falanu disabled if the quest is refused.
#### Lead the Pilgrim to Koal Cave

Disables Fonus Rathryon after the player enters Koal Cave again so he doesn't hang around there forever.
* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
Fonus Rathryon will relocate to Gnisis Temple upon completion of the quest.
#### Viatrix, The Annoying Pilgrim

* [**GAMA - Go Away Move Along**](https://www.nexusmods.com/morrowind/mods/46797) by l1lartur0
